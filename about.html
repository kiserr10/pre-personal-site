A Little More About Me:

As I mentioned earlier, I couldn’t be more excited to be taking this course and to be starting this next chapter of my life. I feel that development will be the perfect outlet to express my creativity. Programming is the core of all technology and I love the idea that you can create something from absolutely nothing. I have known about Galvanize for quite some time and have had a few friends go through the program as well. Earlier this year I took the Immersive Prep Workshop through Galvanize and got a taste of what programming and web development is all about. I was shocked and excited to say the least. I figured the time was right and well, here I am. I’m very excited to be joining the family and to start learning some serious code! After graduating I hope to use the new skills to explore new options and creative avenues. I would love to work for a company that is making or doing something new and out of the ordinary. I hope I will have the tools and a new set of competencies to become a better problem solver and creator of opportunity. The possibilities are truly endless.
