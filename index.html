A Little About Me:

What’s going on everyone! My name is Ross and I couldn’t be any more excited to be spending the next six months with all of you. I am originally from Boston, MA and I moved out here in 2008 to attend the University of Colorado at Boulder. After graduating I moved down to Denver and don’t see myself leaving any time soon. I absolutely love it here. My parents still live in Boston, and my little sister goes to school in Vermont. Over the last four and a half years I have been working at a restaurant downtown while pursuing a passion for music. I produce and perform underground dance music and co-founded a record label with some friends a few years ago. Other passions of mine include climbing, cooking and design.
